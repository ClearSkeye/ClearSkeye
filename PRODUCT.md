# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Primary users are prospective clients, partners, investors, and recruits evaluating the holding company and its portfolio. They arrive to understand the relationship between the parent brand and each company in the Skeye ecosystem.

## Product Purpose
This site presents one holding company and its group of brands under the Sky (Skeye) identity, including Nightskeye, Clearskeye, Blueskeye, Greyskeye, and Aiskeye. Success means visitors quickly understand the portfolio structure, trust the parent brand, and move toward a clear next action such as contact, partnership inquiry, or company-level exploration.

## Brand Personality
Minimal, technical, premium. The tone is clear and confident, with restrained language and strong clarity over hype.

## Anti-references
Avoid sterile corporate SaaS patterns, generic template hero sections, and overused enterprise stock visual language that makes the brand system feel interchangeable.

## Design Principles
1. One system, many companies: make the parent identity legible while preserving distinct portfolio entities.
2. Clarity before decoration: communicate structure, purpose, and trust signals with minimal cognitive load.
3. Premium through restraint: use confident hierarchy, spacing, and copy precision instead of visual noise.
4. Technical credibility, human readability: keep messaging precise without becoming cold or jargon-heavy.
5. Progressive depth: support fast scanning first, then allow deeper exploration into each company narrative.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Target WCAG 2.2 AA as the baseline. Ensure keyboard navigability, strong contrast, readable type hierarchy, and respectful motion defaults for users who prefer reduced motion.
